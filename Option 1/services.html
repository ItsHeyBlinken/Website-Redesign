Our Services

Comprehensive drilling solutions for geotechnical and environmental projects

Monitor Well Installation and Repair

W.E.S.T. Drilling specializes in the professional installation and repair of monitor wells for environmental monitoring and remediation projects. Our experienced team ensures proper well construction, placement, and maintenance to meet regulatory requirements and project specifications.

We utilize modern drilling techniques and equipment to install monitor wells that provide accurate groundwater sampling and monitoring capabilities for your environmental projects.

Environmental Sampling

Our environmental sampling services provide accurate and reliable data collection for soil, groundwater, and vapor phase analysis. We follow strict protocols to ensure sample integrity and regulatory compliance.

Our team is trained in proper sampling techniques and chain of custody procedures to maintain the highest quality standards for your environmental assessment and remediation projects.

Remediation System Installation

W.E.S.T. Drilling installs injection systems and other remediation technologies for environmental cleanup projects. Our expertise includes the installation of various remediation systems designed to treat contaminated soil and groundwater.

We work closely with environmental engineers and consultants to ensure proper system installation and optimal performance for your remediation objectives.

Geotechnical Drilling

We provide comprehensive geotechnical drilling services for foundation analysis, soil exploration, and construction projects. Our services support engineering design and construction planning through accurate subsurface investigation.

Our geotechnical drilling capabilities include soil sampling, rock coring, and in-situ testing to provide the data needed for your engineering and construction projects.

Hydro Vac Services (Potholing)

W.E.S.T. Drilling offers Hydro Vac services using either water or air media for safe and precise utility location and excavation. This non-destructive method allows for accurate utility identification without damage to existing infrastructure.

Our Hydro Vac services are ideal for projects requiring precise excavation around utilities, potholing for utility verification, and safe excavation in sensitive areas.

Concrete Saw Cutting

We provide professional concrete saw cutting services with precision and efficiency. Our equipment includes saws capable of cutting up to 7-5/8 inches deep, suitable for various concrete cutting applications.

Our concrete saw cutting services support construction, demolition, and remediation projects requiring precise concrete removal or modification.
