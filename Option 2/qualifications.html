Qualifications & Certifications

W.E.S.T. Drilling is committed to safety, quality, and regulatory compliance

e-RAILSAFE

W.E.S.T. Drilling participates in e-RAILSAFE, a Workforce Safety and Security Management program for Class 1 Railroads and the businesses that serve them. This certification demonstrates our commitment to safety and security standards required for railroad industry work.

eVerifile

W.E.S.T. Drilling is registered with eVerifile, the government standard in contractor screening for the railroad industry. This registration ensures we meet the highest standards for contractor verification and compliance.

ISNetworld

W.E.S.T. Drilling is registered with ISNetworld, a leading contractor management platform that verifies our safety, quality, and compliance credentials. This registration allows us to work with major clients who require ISNetworld certification.

SafeLand USA

Several of our drilling personnel have completed the SafeLand USA safety training program. SafeLand USA provides comprehensive safety training specific to the oil and gas industry, ensuring our team is well-prepared for industry-specific safety requirements.

TXDOT Pre-Certification

W.E.S.T. Drilling is TXDOT Pre-Certified for Soil Exploration Services (Category 14.1.1). This pre-certification demonstrates our qualifications to perform soil exploration services for Texas Department of Transportation projects.

Licensed & Insured

W.E.S.T. Drilling maintains all required licenses and insurance coverage. Our team is fully licensed and our operations are covered by comprehensive insurance policies to protect our clients and projects.

Our Commitment

Since our founding in 1997, W.E.S.T. Drilling has maintained a strong commitment to safety, quality, and regulatory compliance. We understand that our certifications and qualifications are not just credentials, but a reflection of our dedication to excellence in every project we undertake.

Our participation in these programs ensures that we stay current with industry best practices, safety standards, and regulatory requirements. This commitment benefits our clients by providing assurance that we operate at the highest levels of safety and professionalism.
