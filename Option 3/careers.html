Careers

Join the WEST Drilling team

Work With Us

WEST Drilling is always looking for skilled and dedicated professionals to join our team. Since 1997, we have built a reputation for excellence in geotechnical and environmental drilling services, and we're looking for team members who share our commitment to quality, safety, and professionalism.

We offer competitive compensation, opportunities for professional growth, and a supportive work environment where your skills and experience are valued.

What We're Looking For

Drilling Professionals

Experienced drillers with knowledge of geotechnical and environmental drilling techniques, equipment operation, and safety protocols.

Safety-Focused Individuals

Team members who prioritize safety and are committed to maintaining safe work practices and environments.

Reliable Team Players

Dedicated professionals who show up on time, work well with others, and take pride in their work.

Continuous Learners

Individuals who are committed to ongoing training and professional development to stay current with industry best practices.
