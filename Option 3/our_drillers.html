Our Team

WEST Drilling's experienced and highly trained professionals are dedicated to providing you with dependable and cost-effective geotechnical and environmental drilling services. Our team brings years of combined experience in the drilling industry, ensuring that every project is completed with the highest standards of quality and safety.

Our drilling personnel are not only skilled in their craft but also committed to ongoing training and professional development. Several of our team members have completed the SafeLand USA safety training program, demonstrating our commitment to safety excellence.

Why Our Team Stands Out

Experience

Our team brings years of hands-on experience in geotechnical and environmental drilling, ensuring quality results on every project.

Training

Continuous training and professional development keep our team current with the latest techniques, technologies, and safety standards.

Safety

Safety is our top priority. Our team is trained in industry safety standards and committed to maintaining a safe work environment.

Reliability
